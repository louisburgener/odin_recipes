Louis' Cereal Recipe

Cereal, an all time classic. Quick, somewhat nutritious, and filling to a certain extent.

Louis, why the heck are you making a recipe for cereal?! Well, some of you sickos think you put the milk into the bowl first!

Ingredients:

  • Any kind of milk (literally, if you wanna use chocolate milk I won't judge)
  • Your preferred brand of cereal

Steps:

  1. Pour cereal into bowl
  2. Pour milk into bowl
  3. Amount of milk has some controversy stored in it, some people prefer to drench the cereal in the milk and other prefer to use the milk sparingly. Either way, you cannot go wrong.

Louis' Breakfast Burrito Recipe

The coveted Breakfast Burrito, easy, quick, and delicious.

Allow this recipe to provide with an amazingly simple and delicious burrito, perfect to start your day with.

Ingredients:

  • Two to three eggs
  • One burrito sized flour tortilla
  • Salt and pepper
  • Butter
  • Your preferred hot sauce
  • Walmart hasbrown patties
  • Bacon bits
  1. Heat up a non stick skillet
  2. Throw your hashbrown patties in while your skillet is heating up
  3. As the patties are heating up, begin to seperate them into smaller segments
  4. As your hasbrowns cook, crack those eggs and begin to scramble them
  5. Eggs scrambled, let those hasbrowns cook to your preferred amount of crisp
  6. Once your hashbrowns are cooked thoroughly to your liking, place them in the oven to keep warm
  7. Throw some butter on the skillit and cook those eggs up
  8. Place hashbrowns and egg on the tortilla, season with salt, pepper, and hotsauce
  9. Roll up the burrito and enjoy!
